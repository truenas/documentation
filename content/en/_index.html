Documentation Hub

TrueNAS® is an operating system that is designed to be the most effective and time-efficient solution for storing, securing, and sharing data over a network. It is the simplest way to create a safe, centralized, and easily accessible place for your data.

The articles on this website are designed to introduce TrueNAS and guide you through the process of installing, configuring, and using the software. Reference materials about additional TrueNAS software and hardware products, and tutorials about contributing documentation are also available.
